The Impact and Potential of Quick Response (QR) Codes in Healthcare: A Comprehensive Review

Proceedings of the Human Factors and Ergonomics Society Annual Meeting
November 4, 2024
In today's rapidly evolving healthcare landscape, the integration of innovative technologies is becoming increasingly essential to enhance patient care, medication and overall healthcare outcomes. Through a review of 30 studies, we uncover the significant impact of integrating QR codes into healthcare systems. Notable findings include the role of QR codes in ensuring medication safety and advancing education as well as training within healthcare settings.
