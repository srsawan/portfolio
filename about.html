My Journey

My engineering journey began in the high-octane world of motorsport through SAE Baja, where I learned firsthand how critical supply chain reliability is to operational success. Managing part flows and dealing with late deliveries taught me valuable lessons about the ripple effects of inefficiencies.

Since then, I've transitioned from the racetrack to the shop floor, leading Lean and Six Sigma projects that reduce downtime and variability. Whether I'm optimizing a CNC workflow, analyzing inventory data, or redesigning documentation systems, I approach every challenge with curiosity, structure, and a strong sense of ownership.

My diverse experience spans manufacturing, healthcare UX research, and data analytics, giving me a unique perspective on how engineering principles apply across different industries. I thrive in roles where theoretical knowledge meets practical execution.
